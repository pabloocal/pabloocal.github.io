Description

The Special Session on Applied Category Theory is a special session organized by the participants of the Mathematics Research Communities program on Applied Category Theory, and run by the American Mathemtical Society. The goal of the special session is to provide opportunities for collaboration and community building, bringing together the participants of the Mathematics Research Community as well as outside speakers to share their research.

The special session will be held on

  • Thursday January 5 2023, from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm,
at the John B. Hynes Veterans Memorial Convention Center.

The special session will consist on 14 talks by invited speakers, each talk being 20 minutes long with 10 minutes for questions, as well as a 60 minute long poster session. The speakers are a diverse representation of the members of the Mathematics Research Community on Applied Category Theory, as well as upcoming and established researchers in the field.
