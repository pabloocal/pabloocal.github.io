Local Information

The venue is the Faculty of Science of the Sugimoto Campus of the Osaka Metropolitan University. It should not be confused with Osaka University. The venue is within a 5 minute walk of Sugimotocho Station.

The accommodations we recommend are the Osaka Metropolitan University Guest House, the Kansai Kenshu Center, or a hotel near Tennoji Station. The Osaka Metropolitan University Guest House and the Kansai Kenshu Center are within a 10 minute walk of the venue. Tennoji Station is within a 20 minute commute of the venue.

The airport we recommend is Kansai International Airport (KIX). It is also possible to use Osaka Itami Airport (ITM), although we discourage it. Do not use Haneda Airport (HND), also known as Tokyo International Airport, nor Narita International Airport (NRT), also known as Tokyo-Narita International Airport.

If you will be commuting daily by bus or train from your accommodation to the venue, we recommend you obtain an ICOCA transit card.
